# Outpitch

## What it is

AI-powered job outreach for people actively searching for their next role. Outpitch discovers companies hiring for your target role, surfaces decision-maker contacts, drafts personalized emails, and tracks outreach — with persistent memory via Cognee.

## Who it's for

Job seekers who are tired of spray-and-pray applications and want a focused, intelligent outreach workflow.

## Register

product

## Core differentiator

Cognee knowledge graph stores profile, preferences, company research, and conversation history — so recommendations and drafts improve across sessions instead of resetting every time.

- Marketing landing (`/`)
- App: Chat, Companies, Outreach, Settings
- Onboarding, Sign-in, Sign-up
